How to use the To-Do List?

Step 1: Click the the botton.
Step 2: Once you are redirected to the 'To-Do List' page, you can see there the To-Do List.
Step 3: Click the "Enter task..." button.
Step 4: Once you enter your task, you can click the "Add Task" button to add your task to the To-Do List.
Step 5: You can now see the task you've entered.
Step 6: If you accidentally add a wrong task or when you accomplish the task, you can easily remove it by clicking the "Remove" button.
